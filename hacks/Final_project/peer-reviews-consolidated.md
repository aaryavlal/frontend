## Project Overview

**Our Project:** Interactive educational platform teaching computing concepts through gamified modules including:
- GPU Assembly Simulator (Sequential/Parallel/Distributed computing)
- AI Digit Recognizer (Machine Learning, CNN)
- Speedup Calculator (Amdahl's Law, Parallel Processing)
- Computing Models Interactive Lessons

## Conclusion

Our peer review process highlighted several key strengths in our project:
- Strong technical implementation and interactivity
- Good UI design fundamentals
- High educational value potential

The constructive feedback centered around:
- Making the educational purpose more explicit
- Improving consistency across modules
- Better onboarding and contextual guidance

We're actively addressing these items and grateful for the detailed feedback from our peers. Special thanks to all teams who participated in the crossover review process!
